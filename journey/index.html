My Programming Journey

Primary School

My programming journey started in primary school, year 3. I was minding my own business at lunchtime - probably playing video games in the computer lab, when my friend showed me Scratch . I was awestruck about how you could make your own quizzes and games with such a simple, easy to use interface. I continued messing with Scratch, accidentally causing a few BSODs along the way, until Year 6, when I was starting to find the experience somewhat limiting compared to what I wanted to program.
